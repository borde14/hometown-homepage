Top three activities to do at Navi Mumbai

Attend a show or a concert

Vishnudas Bhave Natyagruha in Vashi is a great place to enjoy concerts and spend your night out.

Go for a picnic in Flamingo Sanctuary

Thane Creek Flamingo Sanctuary is perfect for piknic, or getting Flamingo bird knowledge.

Play sports at the Sports Stadium

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights at DY Patil Sports Stadium
